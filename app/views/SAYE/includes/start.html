Use this service to submit your return at the end of the tax year. This service replaces form 34.

Before you start

The company secretary or the person acting as the company secretary must have approved this return and must also agree to any declaration you make within this service.

Depending on your circumstances, you may need:

  • to record any events related to the scheme in a spreadsheet (.ods format) or comma separated values (.csv) file
  • the scheme organiser’s company name and address, Company Registration Number and Corporation Tax Reference (and those of any participating group scheme members)
  • to know any alterations made to key features of the scheme and know whether there’s been a variation in the share capital

If relevant:

  • .ods files used to record events must be no larger than 10MB
  • .csv files used to record events must be no larger than 1000MB
  • group scheme member details can be entered manually or uploaded in a .csv file

Contact us on our feedback page if the files you use to record events are larger than the sizes specified and we’ll help you to upload them.
